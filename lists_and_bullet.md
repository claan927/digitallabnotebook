# List and bullet points 

To create lists, begin the line with an asterisk, or minus:

* item 1
* item 2
* item 3 

This is with minus:

- item 1
- item 2
- item 3 

You can make sub points with 2 spaces bfore a point:

* item 1 
  * sub-item 1
  * sub-item 2
* item 2

If you want to create a numbered list: 
1. item 1
1. item 2
1. item 3
